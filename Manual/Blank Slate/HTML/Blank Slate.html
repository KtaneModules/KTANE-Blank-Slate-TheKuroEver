On the Subject of Blank Slates

This. Here.

A Blank Slate module initially appears blank. Hovering over the module will reveal the sound of eight touch-sensitive regions.To begin the module, press any of the eight regions.

After a region is pressed, something will happen. Find the section in the manual based on what happened, and follow the rules according to that section to find the next region to press. Repeat this process until eight rules have been followed. The module will then solve.

If an incorrect region is pressed, a strike is issued, but the process for finding the next region to press will not change unless otherwise stated.
